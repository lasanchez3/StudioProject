ABOUT US

Sound of Palenque is a vibrant Colombian band born on the sun-soaked shores of the country’s Caribbean coast. Deeply inspired by their roots, the band blends traditional tropical rhythms like cumbia, champeta, and tambor with modern electronic beats, creating a sound that celebrates Colombia’s rich cultural diversity.

The group is composed of Lucía Márquez (lead vocalist), Daniel “Tambo” Torres (percussionist), and Fernando “Nando” Cruz (bass and guitar). Each member brings a unique flavor to the music, combining ancestral sounds with fresh, innovative energy.

With their debut album, Raíces y Colores (Roots and Colors), the band pays tribute to the Palenqueras—the iconic women of Cartagena known for their brightly colored dresses, tropical fruit baskets, and relentless resilience. These women symbolize strength, culture, and tradition, embodying the essence of Colombia’s history and the vibrant spirit of its people.
